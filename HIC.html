Hardware Identification Chart




Video Capture Card Converts anolog video to digital video.
Graphics Card The use of the graphics card is to operate what is on your screen.
Power Supply The uses of a Power Supply are to convert AC power to DC power. To use this DC energy to power the motherboard and other appliances. The last use of the power supply is to provide a cooling effect for parts.
CD Disk Driver The purpose of the CD Driver is to store computer data. For example, it can store audio files like music.
Motherboard The motherboard holds all of the crucial parts of a computer together.
